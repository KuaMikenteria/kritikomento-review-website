About Kritikomento

Kritikomento emerged from the shadows in 2020, born from the collective disillusionment of a group of artists, writers, and film enthusiasts. In a world increasingly sanitized and filtered, we sought to create a space that embraces the raw, the gritty, and the uncomfortably real.

Our mission is to peel back the layers of artifice in cinema, literature, and art, exposing the beating, sometimes bleeding heart of human creativity. We believe that true art doesn't shy away from the darkness - it illuminates it.

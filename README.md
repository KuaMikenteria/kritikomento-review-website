# 🎭 Kritikomento – Dark Critiques of Cinema, Literature & Art

**Kritikomento** is a static web platform dedicated to exploring the gritty, realistic, and often unsettling side of creative works. It provides a space for deep-dive reviews of movies, books, and visual art, all wrapped in a sleek, dark‑themed interface. The name is a portmanteau of the Filipino word *“kritiko”* (critic) and *“komento”* (comment) – reflecting its mission to spark thoughtful, unfiltered discussion.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Three Core Categories** – Movies, Books, and Art, each with a dedicated page.
- **Rich Review Cards** – Each entry includes a cover image, title, creator, and a concise critical summary, plus a rating.
- **Submit Your Own Critique** – A user‑friendly form that lets anyone contribute their own review (static demo).
- **Responsive & Accessible** – Adapts seamlessly to desktop, tablet, and mobile, with reduced‑motion support.
- **Modern Interactions** – Smooth fade‑in on page load, scroll‑triggered animations, and polished hover effects for all interactive elements.
- **Dropdown Navigation** – Quick access to sub‑categories directly from the header.

## 🖥️ How It Works – User Flow

1. **Landing** – The user sees the home page with an overview of the site’s purpose.
2. **Browse** – From the navigation bar, they can jump to **About**, **Categories**, or **Send Review**. The **Categories** dropdown also offers direct links to Movies, Books, and Art.
3. **Explore** – On a category page, reviews appear as a grid of cards. Each card gives a quick glance at the work, the creator, and a critical snippet. A star rating provides an at‑a‑glance assessment.
4. **Read More** – While the site is static, the layout is designed for future expansion: each card could link to a full‑page review.
5. **Contribute** – The **Send Review** form lets users submit their own critiques. All fields are validated (HTML5 required) and ready for backend integration.
